Read the discussion on the player committee page regarding the item stock update - it's somewhat enlightening, although there is no specific reference to how the restocking works.

It does however hint at how the availability of stock is currently calculated based on percentage of time (overall) that the stock is available (the example used was 80%).

When taking that into account it does make sense that if it takes a long time for an item to run out of stock in a particular country then it will take longer for it to restock (eg. based on that example of 80% availability over time then if it takes 24 hours for stock of a particular item to sell out then it would take about 5 hours for it to restock again).

I don't necessarily think that the limited stock levels are the real issue - it's more that players are travelling when there is no stock available is there is not going to be a restock for a while.

If we can nail the restocking pattern down then with a little bit of cooperation amongst flyers (perhaps the last person to buy before the stock level hits zero posts in the forums or updates the IRC bot in #drugden) then we can figure out when the restocking is likely to happen.

Probably not as much of an issue for the closer countries that have stock selling out faster, but for the longer trips we could anticipate when stock will become available again and adjust our travel schedule accordingly.
